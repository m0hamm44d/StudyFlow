About StudyFlow

Learn more about our mission and the team behind the platform

Our Mission

At StudyFlow, we believe that learning should be engaging, flexible, and accessible. Our platform equips students with AI-driven tools, helping them create interactive flashcards, quizzes, and mind maps from their own notes or from community-shared resources.

By using pomodoro timers, we empower students to maximize retention and achieve their academic goals more efficiently.
